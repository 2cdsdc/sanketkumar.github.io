V6 Engine Assembly

This project focuses on the complete CAD design and assembly of a V6 internal combustion engine, emphasizing parametric modeling, motion analysis, and assembly validation.

Project Objective

To design a fully functional V6 engine assembly with realistic constraints, ensuring proper component interaction and motion.

Tools & Technologies

SolidWorks, Parametric Modeling, Assembly Constraints, Motion Study

My Role

End-to-end design including part modeling, assembly, interference checking, and motion validation.

Engineering Approach


Each engine component was modeled parametrically to allow easy design modifications. Proper mates and constraints were applied to simulate real-world mechanical behavior. Motion studies were conducted to analyze piston-crank interactions and clearance.

Design Visuals


Results & Learnings


The final assembly successfully demonstrated smooth mechanical operation with no interferences. This project strengthened my understanding of complex assemblies, mechanical constraints, and real-world design considerations.
